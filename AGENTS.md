This project is HDP(K) LTD ERP System.

Tech Stack:
- Backend: Laravel 10
- Frontend: Vue 3 + Vite

Core Modules:
- POS
- Inventory
- Accounting & Finance
- Manufacturing
- HR & Payroll
- Reports & Analytics

Security:
- Numeric obfuscation: stored = real/3 + 5
- Device-based access control (trusted devices)
- Role-Based Access (Admin, Manager, Clerk)

Special Rule:
- Purchases & Opening Stock may be funded using CAPITAL as a payment method.
